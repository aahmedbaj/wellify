Sleep Tips

Sleep smart, live better - simple habits for a healthier you.

Why is Sleep Important?

Good sleep keeps your mind sharp, your body energized, and your mood balanced. Without enough rest, your focus, productivity, and health can suffer.

Recommended Sleep Hours

Age Group Hours per Night
Infants (0-12 months) 14-17 hours
Teenagers (13-18 years) 8-10 hours
Adults (18-64 years) 7-9 hours
Seniors (65+ years) 7-8 hours

A calm sleep environment makes all the difference.

Simple Tips for Better Sleep

  • Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day.
  • Keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool.
  • Avoid screens at least 1 hour before bed.
  • Limit caffeine in the afternoon.
  • Exercise regularly, but not too close to bedtime.

Do's and Don'ts

Do:

  • Read a book before bed
  • Drink water (not soda!)
  • Relax with calming music

Don't:

  • Use your phone in bed
  • Drink coffee late at night
  • Nap for too long in the afternoon
