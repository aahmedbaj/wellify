BMI Reader

Learn what BMI is, how to calculate it, and how to interpret your results.

What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index.
It is a simple number that uses

  • Height
  • Weight

to help determine if you are underweight, at a healthy weight, overweight, or obese.

How to Calculate BMI?

The formula is: BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ [Height (m)]²

Example: A person who weighs 70 kg and is 1.75 m tall has a BMI of:
70 ÷ (1.75 x 1.75) = 22.9

How to Read the Result

Once you calculate your BMI, compare it with the ranges below:

BMI Range Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30 and above Obese
